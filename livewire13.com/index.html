Maquina Maestro

Event Description:

Maquina maestro is a popular event conducted by EEE. The goal of this event is to analyze the skill of the competitors in the field of electrical machines in both theoretical as well as practical aspects.
Rounds: 2
Round 1: Objective Test and Reasoning Round
- First is the objective test and reasoning round where the conceptual skills is tested with 45 objective questions and 5 reasoning questions in the duration of 1hr.
Round 2: Object identification and description round
- In the object identification round,the competitors will be made to analyze the machine parts and other electrical devices in total around 20 objects. After seeing the objects, they have to write the description about it.
Rules:
- Maximum of two participants per team.
- No electronic gadgets (Calculator, mobile phones) are allowed.
- Participants would be disqualified if they commit any malpractice.

About KCT

Kumaraguru College of Technology (KCT), Coimbatore is a self – financing Engineering college started in 1984 under the auspices of Ramanandha Adigalar Foundation, a charitable educational trust of Sakthi group. It is located 2kms off the Coimbatore- Mysore National Highway (NH-209) on a serene and well developed campus that spreads over 150 acres.

Under the able guidance and patronage of Arutselvar Dr. N. Mahalingam, Chairman, Sakthi group and the able administration of Dr. B. K. Krishnaraj Vanavarayar, Co- Chairman, the college has developed excellent facilities and resources and stands first in Autonomous ranking. Currently the college offers 12 UG and 14 PG programmes. The college has the approval of the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and all eligible programs have been accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA). ISO 9001:2008 certificate adds another feather to the cap of the institution.

The college has 15 academic departments, 8 research centres, over 279 well qualified teaching faculty, 119 technical staff and 120 office staff. The standard of the education and training imparted by the college is highlighted by the interest shown by the leading companies for on- campus recruitment. Our alumni have done us proud by proving their worth in their chosen field of work.
